<p><p>The Autoantibody Kit market encompasses various testing methods to detect autoantibodies in patients. Indirect Immunofluorescence (IIF) utilizes fluorescently labeled antibodies for visualizing autoantibody presence. Immunodiffusion involves the diffusion of antigens and antibodies in a gel for precipitation reactions. Immunoblotting, or Western blotting, identifies specific proteins through gel electrophoresis and transfer. Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA) quantifies antibodies by utilizing enzyme-linked detection. Other methods may include multiplex assays and bead-based technologies for comprehensive autoantibody profiling.</p></p>
