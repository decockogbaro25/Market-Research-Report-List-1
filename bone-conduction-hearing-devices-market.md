<p><strong>The Bone Conduction Hearing Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Traditional Bone Conduction Hearing Aids</li><li>Bone Anchored Hearing Aids</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bone conduction hearing device market comprises two primary types: Traditional Bone Conduction Hearing Aids and Bone Anchored Hearing Aids (BAHAs). Traditional aids transmit sound vibrations through the skull, making them suitable for individuals with conductive hearing loss. In contrast, BAHAs utilize a surgically implanted titanium screw to directly anchor a device to the skull, enhancing sound transmission and benefiting those with more severe hearing impairments or single-sided deafness. Both types offer alternatives for individuals seeking improved auditory experiences.</p></p>
