<p><p>The high-temperature resistance film capacitors market comprises various types, each suited for specific applications. PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate) film capacitors are known for their reliability and cost-effectiveness, while PEN (Polyethylene Naphthalate) film capacitors offer enhanced thermal stability and electrical properties. PP (Polypropylene) film capacitors are appreciated for their high voltage capabilities and low loss characteristics. PPS (Polyphenylene Sulfide) film capacitors provide excellent thermal resistance and are ideal for harsh environments, making them suitable for demanding industrial applications.</p></p>
