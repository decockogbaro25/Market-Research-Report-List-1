<p><ul><li>Plate Flat Welding Flange</li><li>Flat Welding Flange with Neck</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flat welding flange market is primarily categorized into two types: plate flat welding flanges and flat welding flanges with necks. Plate flat welding flanges are simple, flat discs used to connect pipes or equipment, providing a smooth surface for welding. In contrast, flat welding flanges with necks feature an extended neck that enhances alignment and strength, reducing stress concentrations at the weld joint. Both types are essential in various industries for creating secure, leak-proof connections in piping systems.</p></p>
