<p><p>The stem cell characterization kits market encompasses three primary types: embryonic stem cells (ESCs), mesenchymal stem cells (MSCs), and induced pluripotent stem cells (iPSCs). ESCs are derived from early embryos and have the ability to differentiate into any cell type. MSCs, found in various tissues, are known for their role in tissue repair and regeneration. iPSCs are reprogrammed somatic cells that exhibit pluripotency, making them valuable for research and therapeutic applications. Each type serves unique roles in regenerative medicine and research.</p></p>
