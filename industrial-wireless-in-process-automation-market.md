<p><ul><li>Wireless Local Area Network (WLAN)</li><li>Wireless Personal Area Network (WPAN)</li><li>Satellite (GNSS)</li><li>Low-Power Wide-Area Network (LPWAN)</li><li>Cellular</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial Wireless in Process Automation market encompasses various technologies. Wireless Local Area Networks (WLAN) provide high-speed communication within limited areas, ideal for data-heavy applications. Wireless Personal Area Networks (WPAN) offer short-range connectivity, suitable for connecting sensors and devices. Satellite (GNSS) enables global positioning and communication, supporting remote operations. Low-Power Wide-Area Networks (LPWAN) are designed for long-range, low-power connectivity, perfect for IoT devices. Cellular technology supports mobile connectivity, ensuring reliable communication for remote and distributed systems.</p></p>
