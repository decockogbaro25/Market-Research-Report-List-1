<p><p>The glass fiber cloth market is primarily segmented into three types: E-glass, C-glass, and S-2 glass. E-glass is the most widely used, known for its electrical insulation properties and affordability, making it ideal for construction and automotive applications. C-glass offers enhanced chemical resistance, suitable for corrosive environments, often used in marine and industrial sectors. S-2 glass boasts superior strength and thermal performance, mainly used in aerospace and military applications, where durability and reliability are critical.</p></p>
