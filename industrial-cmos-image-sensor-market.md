<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Industrial CMOS Image Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>FSI</li><li>BSI</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial CMOS image sensor market is primarily categorized into two types: Front-Side Illuminated (FSI) and Back-Side Illuminated (BSI). FSI sensors capture light from the front, making them cost-effective and simpler to manufacture, but they have limitations in low-light performance. BSI sensors, on the other hand, have their circuitry relocated behind the photosensitive area, improving light sensitivity and image quality, especially in challenging lighting conditions. Both types serve various applications in industrial automation, robotics, and machine vision.</p></p>
