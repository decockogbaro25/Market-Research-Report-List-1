<p><strong>The Blood Plasma Fractionators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Membrane Plasma Plasma Fractionator</li><li>Non-Membrane Plasma Fractionator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Blood Plasma Fractionators market is divided into two primary types: membrane and non-membrane plasma fractionators. Membrane plasma fractionators utilize selective filtration techniques, employing membranes to separate components based on size and molecular weight, allowing for efficient purification of plasma proteins. In contrast, non-membrane plasma fractionators rely on various methods like precipitation and centrifugation to isolate plasma components without membrane-based filtration. Each type serves distinct clinical applications, catering to the growing demand for immunoglobulins and other plasma-derived therapies.</p></p>
