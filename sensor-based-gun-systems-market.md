<p><p>The sensor-based gun systems market comprises two main types: sensor-based man-portable guns and sensor-based turret guns. Sensor-based man-portable guns are lightweight, user-friendly firearms equipped with advanced sensors for improved targeting and accuracy, often used by individual soldiers or special forces. In contrast, sensor-based turret guns are mounted systems that utilize sensors for automated targeting and fire control, commonly deployed in military vehicles, naval vessels, or fixed installations to enhance operational efficiency and response times in various combat scenarios.</p></p>
