<p><p>The glass beads market can be categorized into three primary types: solid, hollow, and others. Solid glass beads are characterized by their dense, non-porous structure, often used in decorative applications and crafts due to their weight and durability. Hollow glass beads, on the other hand, are lightweight and may be utilized in insulation and automotive parts, providing thermal and sound insulation. The "others" category encompasses specialty beads, such as reflective or luminescent varieties, tailored for niche applications in various industries.</p></p>
