<p><p>Ceramic Positive Temperature Coefficient (PTC) thermistors are categorized into two main market types: self-heating mode and sensor mode. In self-heating mode, PTC thermistors generate heat due to current flow, making them suitable for applications like overcurrent protection and self-regulating heating elements. In sensor mode, they operate as temperature sensors, providing precise temperature measurements and enabling temperature control in various electronic devices. Both modes leverage the thermistor's ability to change resistance with temperature variations, enhancing their functionality across industries.</p></p>
