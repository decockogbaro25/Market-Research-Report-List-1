<p><p>The thermoform plastic pharma packaging market includes various polymer types, each offering unique benefits. Polyethylene (PE) is known for its flexibility and moisture barrier properties, making it ideal for drug packaging. Polystyrene (PS) is lightweight and transparent, suitable for rigid containers. Polypropylene (PP) is durable and resistant to chemicals, widely used for closures and containers. Polymethyl Methacrylate (PMMA) provides excellent clarity and impact resistance, often used in blisters and trays. Other materials may include PVC and PET, catering to specific pharmaceutical needs.</p></p>
