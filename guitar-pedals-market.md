<p><p>The guitar pedals market includes various types, each serving distinct functions. Gain effects enhance signal strength and coloration, while compressors level dynamics for a smoother sound. Pitch effects manipulate notes for harmonization or modulation. Volume pedals adjust overall loudness, providing control over dynamics. Filter effects shape frequency response, creating unique tones. Time-based effects, like delays and reverbs, add spatial depth. Additionally, the "Other" category encompasses less common effects, expanding guitarists' creative possibilities and sound exploration.</p></p>
