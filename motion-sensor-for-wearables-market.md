<p><p>The motion sensor market for wearables includes two primary types: accelerometers and gyroscopes. Accelerometers measure linear acceleration across different axes, enabling devices to detect orientation, movement, and activity levels. Gyroscopes, on the other hand, assess rotational movement, helping wearables track changes in direction and angle. Together, these sensors enhance the functionality of fitness trackers, smartwatches, and other wearable devices, contributing to improved user experience in health monitoring, sports performance tracking, and augmented reality applications.</p></p>
