<p><p>The gynecological bed market includes two primary types: hydraulic and electric. Hydraulic gynecological beds use a manual pump system for height adjustment, offering stability and ease of use but requiring physical effort for positioning. Electric gynecological beds, on the other hand, feature motorized adjustments, allowing for precise positioning with minimal manual intervention. This enhances convenience and efficiency for healthcare providers. Both types are essential in gynecological examinations and procedures, catering to varying needs in medical facilities.</p></p>
