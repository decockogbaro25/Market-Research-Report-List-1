Pipe filter membranes are tubular in design, ideal for high flow rates, and suitable for various industrial applications. Plate filter membranes, on the other hand, feature flat structures that provide efficient surface area for filtration, making them suitable for processes requiring precise filtration and lower flow rates. Both types enhance filtration performance in various sectors.</p></p>
