<p><p>Continuous VOC Concentrators are specialized systems designed to capture and concentrate volatile organic compounds from industrial emissions. </p><p>Zeolite VOC Concentrators utilize zeolite materials, which have a porous structure, to selectively adsorb VOCs, providing efficient concentration and regeneration of captured compounds. </p><p>Activated Carbon VOC Concentrators, on the other hand, use activated carbon to adsorb VOCs through a physical process, offering a high surface area for adsorption. Both types are essential for compliance with environmental regulations and for improving air quality in industrial settings.</p></p>
