<p><p>FEVE fluoropolymer coatings are categorized into three primary types based on their solvent content. Solvent-based coatings offer high performance and durability, ideal for harsh environments. They provide excellent adhesion and resistance but can emit volatile organic compounds. Water-based coatings are more environmentally friendly, featuring lower emissions and easier cleanup, making them suitable for indoor applications. Solid coatings, which contain no solvents or water, provide robust protection and are often used in demanding industrial applications due to their resilience.</p></p>
