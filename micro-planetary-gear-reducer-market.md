<p><p>The Micro Planetary Gear Reducer market is categorized into two main types: Internal Meshing and External Gearing. Internal Meshing involves gears that engage within a housing, providing higher torque and compact design, making it ideal for applications requiring space efficiency and enhanced efficiency. Conversely, External Gearing features gears that mesh outside of each other, typically offering simpler designs that are easier to manufacture. Both types are essential for various industrial applications, enabling precise control of speed and torque.</p></p>
