<p><p>The micro and mini sprinklers market features two main types: upright and upside-down sprinklers. Upright sprinklers are designed to project water upwards, providing a gentle distribution that is ideal for delicate crops or close planting. In contrast, upside-down sprinklers are mounted inverted, allowing for precise water application directly to the soil, minimizing evaporation and runoff. Both types enhance water efficiency and optimize irrigation practices, making them suitable for various agricultural and horticultural applications.</p></p>
