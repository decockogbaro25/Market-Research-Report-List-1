<p><p>The electro chlorinator market is primarily segmented into two types: brine systems and seawater systems. Brine systems generate chlorine through the electrolysis of a salt solution, making them suitable for applications where saline water is readily available. In contrast, seawater systems utilize the natural salt content of seawater to produce chlorine, making them ideal for coastal areas. Both systems are used for water disinfection in various industries, including swimming pools, wastewater treatment, and industrial processes, enhancing water quality and safety.</p></p>
