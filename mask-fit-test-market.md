<p><p>The mask fit test market is divided into two main types: qualitative and quantitative tests. Qualitative tests assess the effectiveness of a mask's fit based on subjective responses from the wearer, typically through methods like odor detection. In contrast, quantitative tests provide measurable data on mask performance, often using specialized equipment to evaluate the leakage around the mask. Both testing types are essential for ensuring compliance with safety standards and effective protection in various environments, particularly in healthcare and industrial settings.</p></p>
