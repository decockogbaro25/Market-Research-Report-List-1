<p><p>Thin Layer Chromatography (TLC) is a separation technique used to identify and analyze compounds based on their adsorption to a stationary phase and their movement with a mobile phase. The market is categorized into three main types: Classical TLC, a basic method for qualitative analysis; High-Performance TLC (HPTLC), which offers enhanced resolution and automation for high-throughput applications; and Preparative TLC (PLC), designed for separating larger quantities of substances intended for further analysis or purification.</p></p>
