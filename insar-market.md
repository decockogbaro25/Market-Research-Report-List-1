<p><p>The InSAR market can be segmented into two main types based on the number of Synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR) images used for analysis. The "Two SAR Images" method utilizes a pair of images captured at different times to detect surface changes and monitor deformations. Conversely, the "Multiple SAR Images" approach employs a series of images for more comprehensive analysis, enabling enhanced accuracy in measuring ground movement over time. Both methods are vital for applications like urban planning, infrastructure monitoring, and disaster management.</p></p>
