<p><ul><li>Direct ToF (dToF) Driver IC</li><li>Indirect ToF (dToF) Driver IC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automobile Time-of-Flight (ToF) Driver IC market is divided into two main types: Direct ToF (dToF) driver ICs and Indirect ToF (iToF) driver ICs. Direct ToF driver ICs measure the time taken for light pulses to travel to an object and back, providing accurate distance information for applications like parking assist and collision avoidance. In contrast, Indirect ToF driver ICs utilize phase shifts in modulated light waves to determine distance, often delivering enhanced performance in low-light conditions and complex environments.</p></p>
