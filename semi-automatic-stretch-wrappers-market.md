<p><p>The semi-automatic stretch wrappers market is categorized into three main types: Rotary Stretch Wrapper, Turntable Stretch Wrapper, and Robotic Stretch Wrapper. Rotary Stretch Wrappers utilize a rotating arm to apply stretch film around products, ensuring uniform coverage. Turntable Stretch Wrappers feature a rotating table where products are placed and wrapped as they spin, ideal for stable loads. Robotic Stretch Wrappers employ robotic arms to wrap items, offering flexibility and adaptability for various shapes and sizes, enhancing efficiency in packaging operations.</p></p>
