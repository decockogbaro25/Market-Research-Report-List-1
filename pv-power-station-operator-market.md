<p><ul><li>On-grid PV Power Station</li><li>Off Grid PV Power Station</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PV power station operators engage in two main market types: On-grid and Off-grid systems. On-grid PV power stations are connected to the electrical grid, allowing operators to sell excess electricity back to the utility, benefiting from incentives and stable revenue streams. In contrast, Off-grid PV power stations operate independently from the grid, providing power in remote areas where grid access is unavailable. They typically require energy storage solutions, enabling self-sufficiency but may involve higher initial investment and maintenance costs.</p></p>
