<p><strong>The Stream Gauge Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Displacement Stream Gauge</li><li>Ultrasonic Stream Gauge</li><li>Turbine Stream Gauge</li><li>Magnetic Stream Gauge</li><li>Coriolis</li><li>Vortex</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The stream gauge market comprises various technologies for measuring fluid flow. Positive displacement stream gauges capture flow volume directly, while ultrasonic stream gauges use sound waves for non-contact measurements. Turbine stream gauges utilize a spinning rotor for flow rate detection. Magnetic stream gauges measure velocity using magnetic fields, while Coriolis gauges assess flow based on the Coriolis effect. Vortex stream gauges detect flow through vortices generated by obstacles in the fluid. Each type offers unique advantages suited to different applications and conditions.</p></p>
