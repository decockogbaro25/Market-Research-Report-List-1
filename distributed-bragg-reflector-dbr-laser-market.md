<p><p>The Distributed Bragg Reflector (DBR) laser market consists of two primary types: DBR laser diodes and DBR fiber lasers. DBR laser diodes are compact, semiconductor-based devices known for their high efficiency and tunability, commonly used in telecommunications and spectroscopy. In contrast, DBR fiber lasers utilize optical fibers with Bragg gratings to achieve high power output and superior beam quality, making them ideal for industrial, medical, and defense applications. Both types leverage the unique properties of Bragg reflectors to enhance performance.</p></p>
