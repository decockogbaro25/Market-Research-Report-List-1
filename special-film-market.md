<p><p>Special film markets encompass various types designed for specific applications. Barrier film protects contents from moisture and oxygen, essential for food packaging. Conductive film prevents static buildup, beneficial in electronics. Safety and security films enhance glass strength or offer tamper resistance. Anti-fog film is designed for visibility in humid conditions, particularly in packaging and protective gear. Other films cater to niche applications.</p></p>
