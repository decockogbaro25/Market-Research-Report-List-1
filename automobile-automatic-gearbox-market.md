<p><strong>The Automobile Automatic Gearbox Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electric Power Steering Gearbox</li><li>Hydraulic Power Steering Gearbox</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automobile automatic gearbox market primarily features two main types: Electric Power Steering (EPS) Gearbox and Hydraulic Power Steering (HPS) Gearbox. EPS uses electric motors to assist steering, enhancing fuel efficiency and providing precise handling while reducing weight and maintenance costs. In contrast, HPS relies on hydraulic pressure generated by a pump, offering a traditional feel with robust performance. Both systems are crucial in modern vehicles, influencing driver comfort and vehicle dynamics, but they differ in operation, efficiency, and technology integration.</p></p>
