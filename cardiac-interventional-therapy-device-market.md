<p><p>The cardiac interventional therapy device market encompasses various specialized tools used in diagnosing and treating cardiovascular diseases. Cardiology catheters enable access to the heart for procedures. Guidewires facilitate navigation through blood vessels. Balloon catheters are used for angioplasty, helping to widen narrowed arteries. Coronary stents provide structural support post-angioplasty. Fractional flow reserve assesses blood flow in coronary arteries, while intravascular ultrasound (IVUS) offers detailed imaging of vessel walls, enhancing procedural accuracy and patient outcomes.</p></p>
